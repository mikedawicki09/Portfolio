The Project Our System Our Database

CIS Capstone Project

The Computer Information System's Senior capstone was a semester long project in which we built an information system from the ground up for a client in the Harrisonburg community. This capstone included 16 teams that competed to build a system that both best fit the clients needs and added value to their business's every workday.

Our System

Our client's were an auction and moving service's company that needed a system that would replace their current, cultered and out-of-date filing system. We coded our system using Microsoft's ASP.NET, Bootstrap, SQL and C#. My team was chosen among the top two teams to present our system to a judge panel, CIS students, and CIS professors.

Our Database

We coded our relational database with microsoft sql server and developed our database to match our client's needs while also maintaining third normal form and removing any redudancies. From meetings with our client's, we realized that both types of services were very similar which allowed us to integrate generalization into our Entity Relationship Diagram.
