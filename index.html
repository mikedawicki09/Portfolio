Our System

Our clients were an auction and moving service's company that needed a system that would replace their cluttered and out-of-date filing system. We coded our system using Microsoft's ASP.NET, Bootstrap, SQL and C#. My team was chosen among the top two teams to present our system to a judge panel, CIS students, and CIS professors. We coded our relational database with Microsoft SQL Server and developed our database to match our client's needs while also maintaining third normal form and removing any redundancies. From meetings with our client's, we realized that both types of services were very similar which allowed us to integrate generalization into our Entity Relationship Diagram.
